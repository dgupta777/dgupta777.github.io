RAGA NIGHT

Words make you think. Music makes you feel.
A song makes you feel a thought. Singing is in our gene and in human nature.
Singing brings happiness whether you want to do it for fun or professionally.
So take your inner singer out, leap and be a part of what "RAGA NIGHT" has to offer.
So grab the opportunity and showcase your singing talent!

1.) Round 1st members MUST include the team members participating in Bootstrapping.
2.) No classical dance is allowed in this event. however There is no restriction on the theme.
3.) Video Recordings (accompanied by the name of the college, details of participants and scanned copy of college ID cards) must be submitted on this
4.) The min number of participants should be 5 and maximum is 20 { on stage: max(15)}
5.) Pre-recorded music should be brought in a CD/DVD as well as a pen drive in .mp3 format only.
6.) The time limit should not exceed 7+1 minute.
7.) The teams should bring their own portable music system for practice.
8.) Individuality and originality of the dance is encouraged.
9.) Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
10.) Use of only stage is allowed.
11.) The use of fireworks & fire acts, colors, water are not allowed.

All the participants in a group should belong to same technical or management institution. The decision of the judges and the organizing committee will be final.
